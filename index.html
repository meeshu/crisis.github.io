Syrian Refugee Crisis Visualisation

by Meeshu Agnihotri, Nilaksh Das, Omid Elliyoun, Swarnika Prakash

To escape the violence of Syrian Civil War, more than four million Syrian refugees have fled the country to neighboring Turkey, Lebanon, Jordan, and Iraq, while thousands have also ended up in more distant countries of the Caucasus, the Persian Gulf, North Africa and Europe. More than double this number are internally displaced within Syria and live in horrific conditions. A number of international and local non-profit agencies are working towards providing refugees a better way to live with the help of international aid.

The following visualization is aimed to help you understand the gravity of the situation, by visualizing the number of violent events and the ensuing increase in number of refugees.
